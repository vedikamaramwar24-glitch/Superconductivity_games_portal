The Science Behind the Games

The Meissner Effect

When a material transitions into a superconducting state, it actively ejects magnetic fields from its interior. This phenomenon is what allows magnets to literally levitate above superconductors!

Zero Electrical Resistance

At critical low temperatures, electrons pair up (Cooper pairs) and move through the material without any collisions or friction. An electric current in a superconductor can flow infinitely without losing energy.
